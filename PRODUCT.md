# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Windows 11 users who want a calm, familiar macOS-style shell without running a heavy browser-based desktop layer. The interface must stay obvious for non-technical users.

## Product Purpose

Replace the visible Windows taskbar with a lightweight native dock and top status bar. Success means it feels integrated with the desktop, stays out of the way, and remains safe to quit or recover after a crash.

## Brand Personality

Quiet, precise, polished. Smooth and premium without looking flashy or themed.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a stretched Windows taskbar, a row of colored letter tiles, a black rectangular slab, or a crowded launcher showing every available app. Avoid boxy panels, heavy borders, noisy labels, and fake glass.

## Design Principles

1. The desktop remains the visual focus.
2. Show fewer, better-presented controls.
3. Use native icons and familiar Windows behavior.
4. Motion communicates hover, launch, focus, or reordering only.
5. Safety and low idle usage are product features.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect Windows reduced-motion and high-contrast preferences. Keep targets keyboard-accessible, preserve clear focus states, and avoid color-only status communication.
